About Me

My Story

Hi, I’m Nicole. I’m a UX/UI designer from Rochester, NY. I am passionate about understanding user needs and creating intuitive and effective digital products.

I haven’t always been in this field. In fact, my career has been spent in the public sector, mostly focused on international development. My interest in technology and how it can change the development landscape came when I worked on Mobile Money Afghanistan - a groundbreaking project that leveraged mobile technology to pay Afghan National Police.

Fast forward a few years, and I put my research and analysis skills to work as an Intelligence Analyst with the Drug Enforcement Administration. I brought my skills and interest in leveraging technology to my office, where I increased our utilization of open-source intelligence and metadata to further investigations.

In 2022, I decided to make the leap into UX design and completed a bootcamp UX Immersion course with CareerFoundry. I also completed a specialization in Frontend Web Development so that I can not only have the ability to research, conceptualize, and design products, but can also play a role in building the things I design. I am passionate about using design to make a positive impact and drive meaningful change. I am excited to bring my diverse background and unique perspective to make a meaningful impact in UX design!
